    Let People Vote

    While the Trump administration and its allies engage in an ongoing assault on voting rights, People Power is going on the offense at the state and local level to expand access to the ballot and make our democracy more representative.

    In all 50 states and the District of Columbia, People Power activists are taking the lead and working to drive concrete policy change. We're fighting for reform in four key areas:

    1. election reforms to update our voting systems and make participation easier
    2. counters to voter suppression schemes designed to block access to the ballot
    3. voter restoration policies that give people who have served their time and are returning to their communities a chance to fully participate in society, and
    4. redistricting reforms to stop corrosive partisan gerrymandering.

    The path to change starts at the polling booth! Get involved by joining People Power and taking action to Let People Vote in your state.
