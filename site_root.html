    Let People Vote

    While the Trump administration and its allies engage in an ongoing assault on voting rights, People Power is going on the offense at the state and local level to expand access to the ballot and make our democracy more representative.

    In all 50 states and the District of Columbia, People Power activists are playing a crucial role in campaigning for voting rights policies tailored to their state and focused on four key areas:

    1. election reforms to update our voting system and make participation easier
    2. counters to voter suppression schemes designed to block access to the ballot
    3. voter restoration policies that give people who have served their time and are returning to their communities a chance to fully participate in society
    4. and redistricting reforms to stop corrosive partisan gerrymandering.

    The path to change starts at the polling booth! The next step to get involved with Let People Vote is to attend a local event with other People Power activists.
